Chicken and Rice with Garlic Sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Rice
  • Garlic cloves
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Turn stove on and melt button in pot
  2. Add 2-3 cloves of garlic into the pot, wait until cloves brown
  3. Add heavy whipping cream into pot, stir occassionally
  4. Cook chicken breasts (in my case, by placing in an air fryer)
  5. When done, dice chicken breasts and add into pot
  6. Cook rice according to instructions on stove
  7. Pour garlic sauce and chicken atop rice, enjoy :)
