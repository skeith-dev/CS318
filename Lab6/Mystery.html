My Favorite Dogs

Mystery

Mystery is my partner and I's dog, who we adopted last fall from the Eau Claire Humane Society. She is currently two years old.

Mystery is very playful, but also very cuddly. She loves to play with her toys, and to go on long bike rides around Eau Claire.

Mystery is still young and mischeivious, but she has a heart of gold and loves my partner and I as much as we love her. That is to say, a lot!
