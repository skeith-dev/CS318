My Favorite Dogs

Ozzy

Ozzy was the Keith family dog until his passing in October of 2021. He was a pug-border terrier mix that we adopted when he was three years old.

Ozzy was very silly, as can be plainly seen by his appearance. He loved napping, begging for food, and going on walks, amongst many other things.

He was truly the best dog that anyone or any family could ask for, and he is missed every day.
